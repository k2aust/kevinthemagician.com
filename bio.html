Kevin The Magician

Kevin has been performing magic professionally for over 10 years, building a reputation across New England for sleight of hand that leaves audiences genuinely speechless.

What started as tricks for friends and family in high school turned into something far bigger. Kevin dedicated himself to creating original magic — tricks no one else was doing — and recently performed his signature effect, Perfect Storm, at a magic convention where it fooled an entire room of professional magicians.

Today Kevin performs for private parties, corporate events, and schools, while also sharing his original creations online with a growing audience across YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. If you want to see magic that's actually impossible, you're in the right place.
