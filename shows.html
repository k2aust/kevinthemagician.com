Kevin The Magician

Birthday Show

This is Kevin's most popular show. The show makes the birthday child feel like the star by having the magic happen in their hand and they get all the credit. Kevin can specialize his magic to suit the birthday child by incorporating their hobbies and interests into the show. The show lasts 30–45 minutes and is action packed with comedy and audience participation, perfect for the whole family.

Birthday Show (Deluxe)

This is the same unforgettable experience you will get with the Birthday Show plus the birthday child will receive a magical gift and learn, one on one, how to perform a magic trick for their friends and family.

Stand Up Show

Magic performed while standing in front of an entire audience. This type of performance is suitable for a small group or a large group if there is proper lighting and amplification. (Kevin can supply a sound system if needed for an additional fee.) The Stand Up Show is perfect for Corporate Events, Banquets, Trade Shows, Private Parties, Schools, Churches, or anything else you can think of. The Stand Up show is best served hot with a side of "OMG that was impossible."

Walk-Around

Kevin's walk-around magic is perfect for larger functions where it would be harder to seat everyone together in one spot. Walk-around magic with Kevin is an intimate magical experience. There is nothing like seeing objects disappear right in front of your eyes or feeling a fork bend inside your hand. WARNING: If you hire Kevin the Magician to perform Walk-Around Magic at your next event, minds will be blown.
